I am broadly interested in machine learning, theoretical computer science, and mathematics. I have an unquenchable thirst for analytical thinking, abstraction, and computational problem solving. I actively pursue independent study in advanced computer science and mathematics, with a focus on the mathematical foundations of computation and intelligent systems. In addition to my academic preparation, I instruct students in mathematics and physics for challenging university entrance exam in Bangladesh. My goal is to conduct graduate research at the nexus of algorithms, machine learning, and the mathematical aspects of computation.
