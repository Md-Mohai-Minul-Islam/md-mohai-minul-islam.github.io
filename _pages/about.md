As a PhD candidate in computer science, I have a keen interest in programming, machine learning, theoretical computer science, and mathematics. I have deep interest in analytical thinking, abstraction, and computational problem solving. I actively pursue in-depth independent study in advanced computer science and mathematics subjects, with a focus on the mathematical underpinnings of computation and intelligent systems. In addition to my academic preparation, I instruct students in mathematics and physics, emphasizing conceptual clarity, rigorous reasoning, and problem-solving techniques for their challenging engineering admission exam in Bangladesh. My goal is to conduct graduate research at the nexus of algorithms, machine learning, and the mathematical underpinnings of computation.
